Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2025

[Turtle Child] (hereinafter referred to as "the App") operates without a separate sign-up process and values users’ privacy while complying with relevant laws. This privacy policy includes the following information:

Article 1 (Personal Information Collected and Purpose of Use)

1. The App collects only the minimal personal information necessary to provide personalized ai-telling services.
- Collected Items: Date of birth, time of birth, gender

2. The collected information is used solely to generate personalized ai content. All data processing and calculations are performed entirely on the user's personal device without passing through any external servers.

Article 2 (Retention and Disposal of Personal Information)

1. The App does not store any personal information on external servers.

2. The information entered by the user is stored only on the user's device to ensure smooth usage of the App. If the user deletes the App, all stored data will be permanently deleted immediately along with it.

Article 3 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

The App does not provide or sell users’ personal information to any third parties. All information remains within the user's device.
